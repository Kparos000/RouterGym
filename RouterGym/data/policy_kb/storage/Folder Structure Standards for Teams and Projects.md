# Folder Structure Standards for Teams and Projects

**Category:** Storage  

## Description
Recommended patterns for how teams should organise files and folders on shared drives and SharePoint.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Team folder is confusing and duplicated”
- “Unsure where to store project documents”
- “Need guidance on new site structure”

## Resolution Steps
1. Provide standard folder templates (e.g., _01-Admin, 02-Working, 03-Archive_).
2. Encourage separation of personal, team, and project data.
3. Align permissions with structure (e.g., confidential vs general folders).
4. Assist requester in restructuring existing content when necessary.
5. Document agreed structure and share with team.

## When to Escalate
- Large reorganisations affecting many users.
- Structures involving sensitive or regulated data.
- Conflicting requirements between departments.
