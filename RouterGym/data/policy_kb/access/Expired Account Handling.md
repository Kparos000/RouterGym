# Expired Account Handling

## Description
Guidance for resolving Expired Account Handling scenarios in the IT service desk context.

## Common Symptoms
- Users report that Expired Account Handling is failing or unavailable.
- Expected access or behavior is blocked after a recent change.
- Multiple tickets reference Expired Account Handling without a clear owner.

## Resolution Steps
1. Review the ticket details, scope, and business impact for Expired Account Handling.
2. Verify user/account state, connectivity, and recent changes tied to the request.
2. Apply the standard fix for Expired Account Handling (reset, reconfigure, or reissue access) and document the action.
2. Test with the requester; update the ticket with outcome and any follow-up tasks.

## When to Escalate
- Issue repeats after standard fix or impacts multiple users.
- Security/compliance risk is suspected or admin rights are required.
- Underlying service dependency is degraded (directory, network, or core app).
