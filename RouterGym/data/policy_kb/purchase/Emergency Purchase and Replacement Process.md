# Emergency Purchase and Replacement Process

**Category:** Purchase  

## Description
Fast-track process for urgent replacements or purchases due to critical failures.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Laptop lost/stolen – need immediate replacement”
- “Critical equipment failure during go-live”
- “Urgent purchase needed for outage”

## Resolution Steps
1. Confirm the incident severity and business impact.
2. Check for available spare equipment before new purchase.
3. Obtain expedited approval from duty manager or incident commander.
4. Arrange same-day or next-day shipping/collection with vendor or local stock.
5. Record reason for emergency purchase and follow up with normal procurement reconciliation.

## When to Escalate
- Security incidents (lost/stolen devices).
- Major incidents with widespread impact.
- Purchases above emergency spend thresholds.
