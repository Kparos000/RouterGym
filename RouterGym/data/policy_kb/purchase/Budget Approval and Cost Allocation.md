# Budget Approval and Cost Allocation

**Category:** Purchase  

## Description
Ensuring IT purchases are correctly budgeted and allocated to the right cost centres.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Need approval code for IT purchase”
- “Charge wrong cost centre”
- “Need to split costs across projects”

## Resolution Steps
1. Confirm requesting department and cost centre.
2. Validate that budget is available for the purchase.
3. Apply correct GL codes and project codes in procurement system.
4. Coordinate with finance if charges need to be moved or split.
5. Document final allocation in ticket and PO notes.

## When to Escalate
- Budget exceeded or unavailable.
- Cross-department cost disputes.
- Large project or capital expenditure approval.
