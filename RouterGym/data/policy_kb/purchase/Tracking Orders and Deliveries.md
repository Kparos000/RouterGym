# Tracking Orders and Deliveries

**Category:** Purchase  

## Description
Helping users track status of hardware and software orders after approval.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Where is my laptop?”
- “Order shipped but not received”
- “Need courier tracking details”

## Resolution Steps
1. Look up PO or order number and current status.
2. Retrieve courier tracking information if available.
3. Confirm shipping address and contact information with user.
4. Escalate delayed shipments to vendor or logistics team.
5. Update ticket with ETA and delivery confirmation.

## When to Escalate
- Lost shipments or repeated delivery failures.
- Critical go-live or new-starter timelines.
- Damaged goods on arrival.
