# Non-IT Requests and Redirection

**Category:** Miscellaneous  

## Description
Requests that belong to HR, Facilities, Finance, or other non-IT departments.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Question about payslip”
- “Office move / seating request”
- “Catering or events queries”

## Resolution Steps
1. Identify which department owns the request.
2. Reassign or close the ticket with clear instructions on where to submit.
3. Provide relevant contact details or portal links.
4. If many such tickets appear, propose a new category or portal form to service management.
5. Keep a note of common non-IT topics for FAQ creation.

## When to Escalate
- Sensitive HR or Finance topics incorrectly routed to IT.
- Repeated mis-routing due to portal design.
- Complaints about lack of visibility after redirection.
