# Local Admin Rights Policy

## Description
Guidance for resolving Local Admin Rights Policy scenarios in the IT service desk context.

## Common Symptoms
- Users report that Local Admin Rights Policy is failing or unavailable.
- Expected access or behavior is blocked after a recent change.
- Multiple tickets reference Local Admin Rights Policy without a clear owner.

## Resolution Steps
1. Review the ticket details, scope, and business impact for Local Admin Rights Policy.
2. Verify user/account state, connectivity, and recent changes tied to the request.
2. Apply the standard fix for Local Admin Rights Policy (reset, reconfigure, or reissue access) and document the action.
2. Test with the requester; update the ticket with outcome and any follow-up tasks.

## When to Escalate
- Issue repeats after standard fix or impacts multiple users.
- Security/compliance risk is suspected or admin rights are required.
- Underlying service dependency is degraded (directory, network, or core app).
