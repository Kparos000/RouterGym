# Docking Station and External Monitor Setup

**Category:** Hardware  

## Description
Issues connecting laptops to docks and configuring external monitors, network, and peripherals.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Nothing works when I plug into the dock”
- “Dock only drives one of my two screens”
- “Ethernet works only when dock is unplugged”

## Resolution Steps
1. Confirm dock model is supported and connected to the correct laptop port (e.g., USB-C/Thunderbolt).
2. Check dock power, cables, and monitor connections; power-cycle dock and laptop.
3. Update dock firmware and drivers according to vendor instructions.
4. Verify OS display settings, audio output, and network adapter priorities when docked.
5. Test dock with another laptop or test laptop on another dock to isolate the issue.
6. Replace faulty docks through Hardware Replacement Process and update asset tracking.

## When to Escalate
- Multiple users report issues with the same dock model.
- Dock firmware update fails or bricks the device.
- Docking issues affecting critical hot-desk or trading floors.
