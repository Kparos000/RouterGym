# HR Data Privacy and Sensitive Information Requests

**Category:** HR Support  

## Description
Handling access requests, corrections, or deletions for personal HR data under privacy regulations.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Employee requests a copy of their HR data”
- “Request to delete old HR records”
- “Question about who can see my data”

## Resolution Steps
1. Verify identity of the requester and confirm what data is requested.
2. Review applicable privacy regulations and internal retention policies.
3. Coordinate with HR, Legal, and Data Protection Officer as needed.
4. Provide approved data exports or written responses via secure channel.
5. Log request and response dates for compliance tracking.

## When to Escalate
- Complex or cross-border data requests.
- Requests to erase data that must be retained by law.
- Suspected data breach or unauthorised access.
