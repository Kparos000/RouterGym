# Expense Claims and Reimbursements

**Category:** HR Support  

## Description
Problems submitting, approving, or paying employee expense claims.

## Typical Ticket Patterns
- “Cannot upload receipts”
- “Expense claim rejected by system”
- “Status remains pending payment”

## Resolution Steps
1. Confirm user has the correct expense role and cost centre.
2. Ensure they are using the current expense tool and template.
3. Check attachment limits and file types for receipts.
4. Review system error messages and approval workflow.
5. Reprocess failed claims when appropriate and confirm status with payroll.

## When to Escalate
- Large or sensitive claims (travel, relocation) blocked.
- Technical issues preventing expense submission for many users.
- Suspected fraud or policy violations.
